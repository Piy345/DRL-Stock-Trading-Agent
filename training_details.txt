================================================================================
DRL ALGORITHM TRAINING SPECIFICATIONS & HYPERPARAMETERS
================================================================================

This document outlines the detailed configuration and parameters used for 
training the Deep Reinforcement Learning agents on the AAPL trading data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. GLOBAL TRAINING SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Environment Setups: RSI Environment, MACD Environment, Ensemble Environment
- Training Length: 40 Epochs (passes over the training dataset)
- Total Timesteps per model: ~89,280 timesteps (40 * size of training data)
- Observation Space: Historical prices, volumes, and technical indicators.
- Action Space: Discrete [0: BUY, 1: SELL, 2: HOLD]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trained on: RSI, MACD, and Ensemble Environments
- Architecture: Multi-Layer Perceptron (MlpPolicy)
- Learning Rate: 0.0003 (3e-4)
- N-Steps (Steps run per update): 2048
- Batch Size: 64
- Epochs (Optimization epochs per update): 10
- Gamma (Discount factor): 0.99 (focuses on long-term rewards)
- Clip Range (Surrogate clipping): 0.2

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. DQN (Deep Q-Network)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trained on: RSI and MACD Environments
- Architecture: Multi-Layer Perceptron (MlpPolicy)
- Learning Rate: 0.001 (1e-3)
- Replay Buffer Size: 100,000 transitions
- Batch Size: 64
- Gamma (Discount factor): 0.99
- Tau (Target network update rate): 1.0 (Hard update)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. A2C (Advantage Actor Critic)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trained on: RSI and MACD Environments
- Architecture: Multi-Layer Perceptron (MlpPolicy)
- Learning Rate: 0.0007 (7e-4)
- N-Steps (Steps run per update): 5
- Gamma (Discount factor): 0.99
- Entropy Coefficient (Exploration bonus): 0.01
- Value Function Coefficient: 0.25
